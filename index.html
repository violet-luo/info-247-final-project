There're many types of work visas

X most common types of work visas are:

  • H-1B: The H-1B visa is a type of work visa that is granted to foreign workers who have a job offer in a specialty occupation in the United States. A specialty occupation is one that requires the theoretical and practical application of a body of highly specialised knowledge, as well as at least a bachelor's degree or its equivalent in the specific specialty. Some examples of specialty occupations include IT, engineering, and mathematics. The H-1B visa is valid for up to three years, and can be extended for up to six years. It is one of the most common types of work visas issued in the United States.
